Join us in Salam Online

Using internet, well-educated and professional teachers and up-to-date contents, Salam Online Farsi School intends to teach Farsi to those who eagerly want to learn Farsi especially those children who were born into Farsi speaking families and not in Farsi speaking countries.

Salam Online Farsi School tries to fulfill the increasing demand of those parents who really want to preserve their Farsi culture and language by teaching or training their children how to speak, read and write in Farsi. A special focus will be on Farsi Dari.
